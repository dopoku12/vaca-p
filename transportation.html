Getting around

Airport Shuttle

Best for hotel and resort guests landing during daytime arrival windows.

  • Shared shuttle every 45 minutes
  • From $18 per guest
  • Stops at Bayview Hotel and Cliffside Resort

Island Bus Routes

A reliable option for hostel guests and travelers who plan around fixed routes.

  • Main line connects harbor, market, and beach zone
  • Day pass from $6
  • Useful for food market and harbor departures
